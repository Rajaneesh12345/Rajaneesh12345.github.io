How it works

Three steps to clarity

1

Select any text

Highlight any word, sentence, or paragraph on any webpage — up to 600 characters.

2

Click or press Alt+E

A small button appears near your selection. Click it, or press Alt+E for an instant popup.

3

Read the explanation

The AI streams back a meaning, tone, and simplified rewrite — in the depth you choose.

Explanation depth

Four modes, one shortcut

Switch modes with a single click. The last mode you used is remembered.

ELI5

Explain Like I'm 5. Quick, simplified — perfect for unfamiliar concepts.

Normal

Everyday reading level. The default mode for most use cases.

Deep

Academic and literary analysis. Full interpretation of meaning and subtext.

Why?

Why did the author write this line? Authorial intent and context.
