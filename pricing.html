Frequently asked questions

What payment methods are accepted?

Payments are processed securely by Paddle, which accepts credit and debit cards, PayPal, and various local payment methods worldwide.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Monthly and yearly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. You will retain Pro access until the end of the current billing period.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes — we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. See our Refund Policy for details.

What currencies are supported?

Prices are listed in Indian Rupees (₹ INR). Paddle automatically converts to your local currency at checkout and handles all applicable taxes.

Does the free plan require a credit card?

No. The free plan is available immediately after installing the Chrome extension — no sign-up or payment required.

Can I upgrade from Free to Pro later?

Yes. You can upgrade at any time from the extension's Settings page under "Upgrade to Pro".
