Frequently asked questions

Do I need to sign in to use the free plan?

No sign-in is needed for ELI5 and Normal modes — you get 5 anonymous uses. After that, a free sign-in with Google unlocks 10 ELI5 & Normal explains/day plus 1 Deep and 1 Why? per day.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Pro and Premium subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. You retain access until the end of the current billing period.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes — we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. See our Refund Policy for details.

What is the difference between Pro and Premium?

Pro gives 100 explains/day with 10 Deep and Why? each. Premium gives 500 explains/day, 75 Deep and Why? each, large PDF context, priority queue, and access to future premium AI models as they are released.

How are explains counted?

Each explanation request counts as one use. Limits reset daily at midnight UTC. Switching between modes on the same selection uses the per-mode cache and does not count extra.

Which payment provider will be used?

Your region is auto-detected. Indian users are directed to Razorpay (UPI, cards, net banking). International users are directed to Paddle (cards, PayPal, local methods). You can also switch providers manually at checkout.
