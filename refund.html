30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We stand behind Explainly. If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, request a full refund within 30 days and we will process it — no questions asked.

This Refund Policy applies to all paid plans of the Explainly Chrome extension: Monthly, Yearly, and Lifetime. Payments are processed by Paddle, our Merchant of Record.

1. Eligibility for a Refund

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • Your refund request is made within 30 days of the original purchase date.
  • You have not already received a refund for a previous purchase of the same plan type.

Refunds are available for all plan types:

  • Monthly: Full refund within 30 days of purchase or renewal.
  • Yearly: Full refund within 30 days of purchase or renewal.
  • Lifetime: Full refund within 30 days of purchase.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at rajaneeshg0@gmail.com with:

  1. The email address used for your Explainly account.
  2. Your order or transaction ID (found in your receipt from Paddle).
  3. The reason for your refund (optional — but helpful for improving the product).

We will respond within 2 business days and process your refund through Paddle.

Request a Refund

3. Refund Processing

  • Refunds are processed by Paddle, our payment processor.
  • Once approved, refunds typically appear within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
  • Currency conversion differences (if any) are handled by Paddle and are not our responsibility.

4. After a Refund

  • Upon approval of a refund, your account will be downgraded to the Free tier.
  • Cloud saves and other Pro features will no longer be accessible, but your saved data will be retained for 30 days in case you resubscribe.
  • Monthly and yearly subscriptions will be cancelled automatically when a refund is issued.

5. Exceptions

We reserve the right to deny a refund request if:

  • The 30-day window has passed.
  • We have reasonable evidence of fraud or abuse (e.g., repeated refund requests across multiple accounts).
  • The account has been suspended for a violation of our Terms of Service.

6. Free Plan

The free plan does not involve any payment, so there is nothing to refund. If you have a question about free plan limitations, please contact us and we will be happy to help.
